About Us

Welcome to Zani's Eatery! Located in Katlehong, Zani's Eatery is your go-to spot for delicious fast food. We serve a variety of mouth-watering dishes made from the freshest ingredients, ensuring quality in every bite.

  • Founded to provide a warm and friendly dining atmosphere, we are committed to quality and customer satisfaction.
  • From hearty burgers to refreshing beverages and delectable desserts, we have something for everyone.
  • We aim to provide an exceptional dining experience, whether you're dining in, taking out, or ordering for delivery.

Conveniently located at 105 Sebobane Street, Monise, Katlehong, Zani's Eatery is easily accessible and ready to serve you. We open at 11 AM daily.

We love hearing from our customers! If you have any questions, feedback, or would like to make a reservation, please feel free to contact us at +27 83 410 3292. For your convenience, you can also find us on delivery platforms like Mr D Food, so you can enjoy our delicious offerings from the comfort of your home.
